THE STORIES WE TELL OURSELVES

One of the most important aspects in personal development is "seeing" the stories we tell ourselves. This is crucial because it's the meaning we give to any circumstance. Every event that happens in our lives is neutral until we give it a meaning. The default thinking/meaning we go to, then defines how we feel and the actions that we take as a result. If we change the story, we change the result. The default thoughts and beliefs are "programs" that run our life. They either support our vision or the sabotage it.

The things that you like in your life, that come with ease, are there because you have "programs" and stories that support their manifestation.
